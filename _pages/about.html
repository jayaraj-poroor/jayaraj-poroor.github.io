Jayaraj is a scientist-engineer with a strong track record of developing viable, strategic technologies from his directed basic research.

Over a career spanning 25 years he has done substantial hands-on work on a wide range of programming languages, tools, and platforms ranging from formal verification tools to Python-based ML frameworks to C and assembly language. His deep systems understanding and strong mathematical thinking enables crafting effective solutions for hard, practical problems.

He has led several large research and development projects, resulting in numerous core technologies ranging from a real-time operating system kernel to programming languages to smart devices and AI algorithms. During the course of his career he has always taken the extra effort to mentor his team members.

He has been a visiting research faculty at Microsoft Research India and his research has been published in leading scientific conferences and journals. His past experience includes setting up a fully grant-supported research centre from ground up at Amrita University, founding an Industrial IoT startup, and corporate experience in India and the US.

His research interests lie in programming languages (PL), systems, IoT, and the intersection of ML and PL.
