Notes
Top-down parsing tends to introduce right-associativity for the operators, i.e., if an operator with same precedence appears on left and right sides of an operand, the operand will associate with the operator on the right side. So 1 - 3 - 2 would be parsed as 1 - (3 - 2), evaluating to 0 rather than the customary -4 (Thanks to Prof. Bharat Jayaraman for pointing this out). Therefore, it is always better to use parenthesis to dis-ambiguate.
