The paper shows that dataframes can be realized as zero-cost, memory-safe abstractions in resource-constrained environments. In order to achieve this we employ:

  • A dataframe type system specified directly on memory addresses.
  • A subsumption relation that relates dataframe fragments with the whole
  • A specialized formal logic (based on separation logic) for reasoning about typing correctness of dataframe operations
